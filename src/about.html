# About ## About me My name is Emil Ernerfeldt and I am a programmer working and living in Stockholm, Sweden. I studied computing science at [Umeå University](http://www.umu.se/english/), where my master thesis was the popular 2D physics sandbox [Phun](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0H5g9VS0ENM). At [Algoryx Simulation](http://www.algoryx.se/) I was the lead programmer for the physics education app [Algodoo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xvAVQ6GEv-E). At [Arrowhead Game Studios](http://arrowheadgamestudios.com/) I did graphics, engine and game programming for [Gauntlet]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gauntlet_(2014_video_game)"). I am currently working with 3D body scanning at [Volumental](https://www.volumental.com/). I am most happy in writing Rust or C++ in Sublime on a Macbook. I am the author of a few popular C++ libraries hosted on [my GitHub page](https://github.com/emilk/), including [Loguru](https://github.com/emilk/loguru), [Configuru](https://github.com/emilk/Configuru) and [emilib](https://github.com/emilk/emilib). I tweet [@ernerfeldt](https://twitter.com/ernerfeldt). You can reach me at emil.ernerfeldt@gmail.com. ## About this blog This is my personal blog, which I use as a pulpit to spread ideas I deem interesting or important. The ideas expressed are that of me, and not that of any employer past or present. I have chosen not to have any advertisements, as I want a nice, clean and fast website.
